How it works

Three checks, not one guess.

Every pick goes through the same process, every time. No shortcuts, no exceptions for a "good feeling" about a match.

01

Real stats, real math

Actual match data — goals, shots, cards, corners, form — run through probability modeling. Not opinions, not vibes. A number comes out, or it doesn't.

02

A second check, not a rubber stamp

Every number the math produces gets reviewed for reasoning holes before it goes anywhere near a pick. If there's real doubt, it's cut here — no partial credit for "pretty confident." This step catches mistakes. It doesn't set your odds.

03

Clear the bar, or it doesn't ship

Only picks that clear our publication threshold are released. The threshold can change without changing this website. Most days that's a handful of picks. Some days it's zero. Both are normal.
